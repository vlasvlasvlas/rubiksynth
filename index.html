RubikSynth

An experimental instrument where self-solving Rubik's cubes generate music in real time.

  • Each color maps to a musical note in the selected scale.
  • Every move triggers a sound — face direction changes the octave and duration.
  • Click any cube to open its synth inspector and customize sound independently.
  • Drag to pan the canvas. Scroll or use +/− to zoom.
